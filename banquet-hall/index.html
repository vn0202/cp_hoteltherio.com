Banquet Hall

Celebrations So Memorable

Deciding on the perfect wedding venue can be a little bit tricky, what with so many options out there. But, if you want your wedding to be absolutely dreamy, then let us help you. Our beautiful banquet area is perfect for playing host to a large number of guests, who will most certainly be impressed with our best class services and gorgeous decorations. And don’t worry, we keep in mind your vision, your requirements, your budget and most important of all.

Hotel The Rio presents wonderful options for wedding feasts, conference packages, state functions or themed events. - With its elaborate multi-cuisine buffets laden with the choicest regional Indian menus and other cuisines. A panel of professional wedding planners/vendors helps to choose the décor and our celebrated Chefs serve matching food for these thematic events.

 

Food and Beverages

Our banquet hall is aesthetically designed with technologically advance sound and lighting systems. The banquet hall is furnished with all the high-end facilities with appealing ambience and artistic interior. The rooms at banquet hall are equipped with all the modern amenities that cater to various events whether private or business.

We give utmost attention to the menu preparation by personally managing the entire menu by food and beverages manager. Our catering services are impeccable as we possess a team of professionals to make all the arrangements. Hotel The Rio have built the reputation for its unique style of services and matchless standards that we offer to our esteemed clients.
